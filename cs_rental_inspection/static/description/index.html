Rental Inspections Module

The Rental Inspections Module is designed to simplify and enhance the management of rental equipment by providing structured inspection workflows. Whether you’re renting out tools, heavy machinery, or other equipment, this module ensures you capture essential details at pickup and return.

Key Features:

  • Create customizable inspection templates for both pickup and return.
  • Automatically link templates to product categories for streamlined operations.
  • Enable photo capture to document equipment condition during inspections.
  • Enhance your team's efficiency with a seamless workflow directly integrated into the Rental process.

Why Choose This Module?

This module resolves a common gap in equipment rental systems by providing a user-friendly inspection process. It reduces the risk of disputes, ensures better record-keeping, and improves client satisfaction through detailed and accurate equipment status reports.
